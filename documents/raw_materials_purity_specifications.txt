MATERIALS REQUIRING ATTENTION:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
✓ EXCELLENT - No Issues:
  • Silica Sand (95.28% SiO₂, very low Fe)
  • Calcite (98% CaCO₃, ultra-pure)
  • Calcined Alumina (99% Al₂O₃, premium)
  • Rutile (97.37% TiO₂, ultra-pure)

✓ GOOD - Minor Considerations:
  • Dolomite (balanced CaO/MgO, good for neutral fluxes)
  • Fluorspar (92.23% CaF₂, but 0.36% S - monitor for low-S grades)
  • Manganese (73.82% MnO₂, 8.61% Fe - typical for Mn ore)
  • DB Magnesite (85.56% MgO, higher SiO₂ but low reactivity)

⚠️ ACCEPTABLE - Formulation Limitations:
  • China Clay (1.5% Fe₂O₃ - avoid in low-iron formulations)
  • Bauxite (4.72% TiO₂ - may affect some specifications)
  • Magnesite (8.41% SiO₂ - moderate, but acceptable)


AWS A5.17 COMPLIANCE STATUS:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ALL materials in database meet AWS A5.17 minimum specifications for their
intended use in standard SAW flux formulations.

For LOW-HYDROGEN (H4/H8) grades, prefer:
  • Calcined Alumina over Bauxite
  • Premium Fluorspar (>97% CaF₂)
  • Rutile for TiO₂ additions
  • Avoid China Clay (higher Fe)
